ABSURD THEATRE
& WHOLE

Riso Zines

  • 2 Softcover Zines
  • A5 format, 8 pgs each
  • Digital illustration, 2 Color Risograph Print
  • Part of the Wisdom Transfer project
  • Ref: POCI- 01-0145- FEDER- 029038
  • https://endlessend.up.pt/wisdomtransfer
    •
  • 2020

Absurd Theatre and Whole are two visual narrative zines inspired by the lives of two recognized contemporary elderly painters who graduated and gave classes at FBAUP, namely, Carlos Carreiro and Graça Morais.
I was invited by professor Rui Vitorino Santos to develop these zines and stand-alone portraits as part of the doctoral project Wisdom Transfer: Towards the scientific inscription of individual legacies in contexts of retirement from art and design higher education and research.
Absurd Theatre takes inspiration from the surrealism of Carlos Carreiro’s work, depicting the daring escape of stylized drawing from the confining realism of the classic art produced in academia.
Whole, on the other hand, explores the reality of a female artist working in a male-domineered, dictatorship-driven Portugal during the 1960s, depicting the struggle between forging one’s own identity as a woman and adhering to stereotypes of being a housewife.
