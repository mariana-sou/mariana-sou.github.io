Fusion Cuisine

Recipe Book

  • Hardcover Cookbook
  • Vertical A4: 21cm x 29,7cm x 2cm
  • Digital illustrations
  • Analog calligraphy (chisel tip markers + indian ink brush)
  • 2018

Fusion Cuisine is a cookbook for fusion cuisine recipes with design inspired by the Polish Poster artistic movement, particularly the works of Henryk Tomaszewsky e Jan Lenica.
The vertical A4 format is meant to refer to the more conventional formats of the poster; the aesthetic of the project, combining both analog calligraphy, digital illustration and typograhy alludes to the diversity of materials and techniques, as well as the variety of approaches and styles inherent to the Polish Poster movement at its height.
The backcover of the book, on the other hand, refers to the aesthetic of family cookbooks, passed down through generations, scribbled over with annotations.
This project is a template for what would be an extensive, markeatable book full of recipes following a specific aesthetic and grid system.
