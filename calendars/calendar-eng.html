ILLUSTRATED
RISO CALENDARS

Curated by Ruben Roxo

  • Risoprinted Illustrated Calendars
  • Curated by Ruben Roxo
  • Graphic Design by Catarina Neves
  • Yearly theme depicted by 12 illustrators
  • 2021 ⟶ "Party", December, Green/Fluorescent Pink
  • 2020 ⟶ "Home", June, Yellow/Green
  • 2019 ⟶ "Dreams and Goals", November, Yellow/Fluorescent Pink
  • 2019 Illustration featured in Clube dos Criativos Expo '19

The Illustrated Calendar is an on-going project created and curated by Ruben Roxo, inviting a selection of 12 young illustrators for each year's edition who follow a specific theme.
It is a limited edition, risoprinted calendar for the art aficionados and collectors. Because of its printing technique, each calendar is a unique piece, featuring 4 combinations of vibrant colors to match the seasons.
The 2021 edition was sponsored by Olmar. Purchase it @ ko-fi.com
