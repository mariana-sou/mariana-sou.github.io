Cooperative Clutter

Audiovisual Sandbox

  • Part of Porto Design Biennale 2019
  • "Y, Designing Portugal" Exhibition
  • Installation + Interactive Computer Program
  • Audiovisual Composition Creation
  • Installation: 2m x 2m x 2m light-proof cube
  • Visual Dev: Mariana Sou, Cristina Neto,
  • Sound Design: Ana Freitas, Mariana Seridónio
  • Programming: Helena Ruão, Francisco Ramos
  • Originally the final project for Multimedia Design class, 4th year @ FBAUP
  • 2019

“Cooperative Clutter” is an installation that consists of an interactive platform that allows the creation of a visual and audio composition, within the space of the screen resorting to an available library of objects.
To enhance the players’ experience, our first priority was to guarantee their immersion, freedom of exploring and the interpretation of the resources that they had laid out for them. On that note, it’s given the player the possibility to head towards various logics to create their composition. For example, they can choose to create a more appealing composition, for its aesthetic visual; a composition that emphasises the sound; a composition that values a storytelling perspective or none of the above.
