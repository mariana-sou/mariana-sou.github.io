Maternity
Ward Mural

Pedro Hispano Hospital

  • Medical Office in Risk Pregnancies’ Wing
  • Pedro Hispano’s Hospital, Matosinhos
  • Mural Painting spanning 3 walls
  • 4 color palette
  • Part of Maternity Ward Rehab Project
  • Mariana Sou, Margarida Ferreira, Lince Rebelo, Cristina Neto, Francisco Ramos, Mariana Seridónio
  • https://noticias.up.pt/estudantes-de-belas-artes-dao-cor-a-nova-ala-do-hospital-pedro-hispano/
    •
  • 2019

This mural was conceived for the rehabilitation and makeover project of Pedro Hispano’s Maternity Ward. The first phase of the project covers the Risk Pregnancies’ Wing, and includes mural paintings for 5 rooms and the central corridor, created by a team of 6 illustrators.
Painted across three walls of the Medical Office room, the mural follows the project-wide briefing of an underwater/sea/ocean theme using the same restricted 4 color palette featured in all murals.
Thank you to those who lent a hand painting our murals: Ana Freitas, Sandro Delgado, Ruben Roxo, Hugo Sá.
