Toca a Roer!

Traditional Picturebook

  • Short Story written by Eugénio Roda
  • Softcover Mono, 25 x 25cm
  • 32 pages
  • Original Artwork: 25 x 50 cm
  • Gouache + Digital Touch-Ups
  • Final Project of Illustration Post-Grad
  • 2020

Toca a Roer was the final project for the main class of the Post-graduate Degree in Illustration I concluded in 2020, at FBAUP. It is a traditional picturebook by design, following the standard number of pages, composition and pacing tropes inherent to the genre.
The concept for the book’s illustrations provides an answer to the challenging questions at the heart of the project’s brief:
⤳ How do you suggest a 3rd interpretation, born from the intersection of the illustrator’s own perspective and the text itself?
⤳ How do you preverse the message of a book filled to the brim with mice and mouse puns, without ever drawing a single mouse?
The final solution meditates on the border between wilful living and societal performance. The original artworks, painted with gouache, focus primarily on ambience, color and analog texture.
