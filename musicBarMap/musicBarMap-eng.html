PORTO
MUSIC BAR MAP

Illustrated Map

  • Horizontal A4: 21 x 29,7 cm
  • Double-sided: Cover / Map
  • Digital Illustration
  • Digital Print on Munken Paper
  • Academic Project
  • 2018

This map presents the location of the most well-known music bars in the city of Porto. Side A, its cover, is meant to depict the characteristics and feel of Porto’s music bars, featuring little easter eggs such as the album covers of the city’s iconic bands (GNR, Ornatos Violeta).
Side B presents a drawn map of the center of the city, playing on the shape of guitar strings, as a continuation of the cover’s aesthetic.
