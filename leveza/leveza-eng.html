ON LOSING MYSELF

GRADUATION BOOK

  • Hardcover Book
  • 3 Color Risograph cover
  • 15,7cm x 22, 3cm x 1,1cm
  • 80 pages
  • Original Illustrations drawn using colored pencils and alcohol-based markers
  • LCD @ FBAUP Graduation Project
  • 2019

On Losing Myself (A Leveza De Me Perder, in portuguese) is a hybrid between a graphic novel and a picturebook. It approaches the core dichotomy of intimacy and isolation through themes such as online relationships, perfectionism, expectations, transition to adulthood, among others, through the use of visual metaphors and, in particular moments, text.
It is an exploration of the possible ways one loses themselves in the modern age in a subjective, non-linear autobiographic lens, told through the perspective of 3 distinct characters, each with their own aesthetic universe and converging story arcs. It features a mobile-only digital chapter, acessible through a QR code, depicting the moment a character disappears from the physical format of the book into the internet.
A revision and english translation of the text is currently in the works, while publishers for the book are being sought after on a national level.
