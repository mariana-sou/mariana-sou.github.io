Jeff Mangum
T-shirt

"Oh Comely" homage

  • Standard EU Unisex M Size Shirt
  • Black & White Digital Transfer Print
  • Academic Project
  • 2018

The briefing for this project was to create a t-shirt for a celebrity, featuring their face and a notable quote. The t-shirt I created depicts Jeff Mangum, the frontman of the band Neutral Milk Hotel.
The design is based on the last verses of the song “Oh Comely”, written into the illustration; as such, his own portrait blends itself with the design and shirt itself.
